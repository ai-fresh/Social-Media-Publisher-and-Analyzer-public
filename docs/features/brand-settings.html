Brand Settings

Configure your thumbnail provider and cover style prompt — located in the Konta view, in the Ustawienia marki section.

Where to find it

Brand settings are part of the Konta view — click Konta in the app sidebar. The Ustawienia marki section appears below the connected accounts list.

Thumbnail provider

A segmented toggle lets you select which AI model generates cover thumbnails:

  • Imagen 4 (Google) — Google's Imagen 4 model via Gemini API. Requires a paid Google AI Studio plan and a GEMINI_API_KEY.
  • DALL-E 3 (OpenAI) — OpenAI's DALL-E 3 model. Requires OPENAI_API_KEY. Output is resized from 1792×1024 to 1280×720.

This setting applies globally to all cover generation across all videos.

Prompt stylu okładki

A large text field where you describe the visual style for all generated thumbnails. This prompt is passed to the image model (Imagen 4 or DALL-E 3) as context for every cover generation request.

Tip: The more specific your style prompt, the more consistent your thumbnails look across videos. Describe colors, typography, mood, lighting, and any visual elements that define your brand.

Example prompts

  • "Dark cinematic background, bold white sans-serif text at the top, neon blue accent lines, high contrast, professional YouTube thumbnail style"
  • "Bright and colorful, flat design, clean white background, bold title text in the center, minimal icons"
  • "9:16 vertical format, gradient background from dark purple to black, large face close-up on the left, title text on the right"
