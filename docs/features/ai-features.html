AI Features

Three-step AI pipeline that converts a raw video into fully described, publish-ready content — transcription, metadata, and cover generation.

AI Pipeline — 3 Steps

Open any video from the library to see the video detail sheet. The AI pipeline is shown as three sequential steps. Each step shows a green checkmark once it's complete. Steps can be triggered individually or all at once via Generate All.

✓

1. Transkrypcja (Whisper)

Converts the video's audio to SRT captions using OpenAI Whisper. The resulting transcript is displayed in the Napisy SRT section at the bottom of the sheet and used as context for all subsequent AI steps.

✓

2. Metadane (GPT-4o)

Generates the video Tytuł (title) and Opis (description) from the transcript. Also generates Hashtagi shown as selectable pills — you can enable or disable individual hashtags before publishing. Brand settings (tone, style prompt) are applied automatically.

✓

3. Okładka (Imagen 4 / DALL-E 3)

Generates a video thumbnail using the selected image model. Before generating, choose the Proporcje (aspect ratio): 9:16, 16:9, or 1:1. The provider (Imagen 4 or DALL-E 3) is set in Brand Settings.

Editable Fields

After AI generation, all fields in the video detail sheet are editable:

  • Tytuł — title text field, editable inline
  • Opis — description text area, editable inline
  • Hashtagi — shown as pills, click to toggle selection
  • Napisy SRT — the raw SRT transcript is visible and editable at the bottom

Click Zapisz to save any manual edits. Click Zamknij to close without saving.

Note: Transcription must complete before metadata generation. Metadata must exist before cover generation. The pipeline enforces this order automatically.

Aspect Ratio Selection

Before generating the cover, select the appropriate aspect ratio using the Proporcje toggle in the video detail sheet:

  • 9:16 — vertical, for TikTok and Instagram Reels
  • 16:9 — landscape, for YouTube
  • 1:1 — square, for Instagram Feed

Napisy SRT

The full SRT transcript is visible in a scrollable text area at the bottom of the video detail sheet. It shows the raw timestamped captions generated by Whisper. This transcript is the source of truth for all AI generation — better audio quality means better generated content.

Backend Endpoints

POST /api/videos/{video_id}/transcribe
POST /api/videos/{video_id}/generate-metadata
POST /api/videos/{video_id}/generate-cover
POST /api/videos/{video_id}/generate-all

The generate-all endpoint chains all three steps sequentially in a background task.
